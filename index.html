Research

We generate evidence on eye health challenges, management strategies and their impacts through clinical and implementation research.

Training

We empower and build the capacity of the community eye health and vision rehabilitation service personnel.

Services

We implement an integrated and comprehensive eye care service delivery programme to reduce the burden avoidable blindness and other non-vision impairing but disabling conditions by promoting awareness and integrating case identification and referral at all levels of Ethiopia’s health care system.

Rehabilitation

We are engaged in vision rehabilitation programmes to restoring functional ability and improving quality of life and independence of individuals with low vision or lost visual function.
